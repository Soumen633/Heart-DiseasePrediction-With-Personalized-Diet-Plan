About CarePulse

Revolutionizing heart health through AI-powered prediction and personalized nutrition

Our Mission

To democratize heart health assessment and provide personalized dietary guidance through cutting-edge machine learning technology, making preventive healthcare accessible to everyone, everywhere.

CarePulse combines the power of artificial intelligence with evidence-based nutritional science to help individuals understand their cardiovascular risk and take proactive steps towards better heart health.

What Makes Us Different

Advanced Model

Our machine learning model is trained on comprehensive cardiovascular datasets with 92% accuracy, analyzing 20 key health parameters to provide reliable predictions.

Personalized Nutrition

Receive customized diet plans based on your specific risk level, dietary preferences, and nutritional needs, created by certified nutritionists.

Privacy First

Your health data is processed securely and never stored permanently. We prioritize your privacy with end-to-end encryption and anonymous processing.
